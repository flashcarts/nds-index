The R4iDSN 3D is a R4i Gold 3DS clone and one of the few non-Original R4 carts to get an official port of Wood R4. While this cart runs Wood R4 1.62, it does feature Real-Time-Save (RTS) support.

### Setup Instructions (Wood R4 1.62)
1. [Format the SD card you are using to FAT32](https://dsi.cfw.guide/sd-card-setup.html)
1. Download the Wood R4 1.62 Kernel
1. Extract the Wood R4 1.62 Kernel .zip file to your SD card root.
1. Place any `.nds` game ROMs you'd like to play into a folder of your choice which isn't the `__rpg` folder.
    - (Optional) You may want to create a `Games` folder to store your ROMs in to keep the root of your SD card organised.
